DnDeck


DnDeck is an unofficial Dungeons & Dragons inspired card game for Android. Create your character, choose their weapons and spells, manage your deck of cards and fight creatures from the whole D&D bestiary. Each playing card suit represents one of your ability, either a weapon or a spell. The bigger the value of the played card, the stronger the ability. Draw cards in your deck or your opponent's, choose three cards to play, and see the result of the turn!


This game is the result of my work for an Android course. We had to create a basic app that used at least one public API. I decided to use the Deck of Cards API and the D&D 5e API to create a basic (and unbalanced) card game. I used the AideDD Website to retrieve the enemies pictures.
