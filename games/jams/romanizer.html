Make sure the phrygian goddess Cybele becomes a part of the Roman Pantheon in this historic management game, and meet flamboyant poets who had a hand in the writings about this little-known episode of roman history. Use your influence wisely to affect the opinions of different parts of roman society and obtain their support, and try to survive the important historic events that will come and disrupt your efforts. Be wary, if part of the roman population is too unhappy with Cybele, she might end up thrown to the lions...


Romanizer was created as a submission to the Scientific Game Jam - Lyon 2021, which had 19 teams work on games based on some of the contestants' theses. This game was created from a thesis dealing with the integration of "oriental cults" into the Roman Pantheon, depicted by different poets. It was awarded the Grand Prix of the jam. I was part of a 6-people team, as lead developer, because my fellow programmer didn't have experience with Monogame. I also took part in the game design discussions, and designed the UI of the game.
