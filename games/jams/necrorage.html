You play as a necrophagous creature named Necrovore looking for meat from corpses.

Your quest for meat takes place in a batteflied opposing two armies. It is the perfect place and moment if you want to feast on fresh corpses! But do be careful, both sides of this battefield want you dead and there are other Necrovores who want to eat as well. Avoid the knights attacks and don't let your meal be stolen!


NecroRage was created as a submission to the Unijam 2021 game jam, with the theme "One man's trash is another man's treasure". It was voted second for Best Game of the jam, and received three jury's favorites. I worked mainly as a gameplay programmer as part of a team of six students. I also helped with game design when we first discussed the game concept, and programmed various other tidbits.
