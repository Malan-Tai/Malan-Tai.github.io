Somnium


In your dreams, no one can hear you scream.

Somnium is a puzzle-horror game where, in an oppressing nightmare, the player has to save a kid from a monster only they know about. To do so, the decorative elements can be interacted with so as to scare the child and make them flee. However, these actions build up their fear, and beware of a heart attack! Will you get to the end of this awful nightmare?
